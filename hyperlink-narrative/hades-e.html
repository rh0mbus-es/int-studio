you make your way through the crowds of shades towards the king.
hades observes the souls impassively, a watchtower over the sea of what once were people.

you inch towards him, the currents of shades impeding your path more as you get closer.

someone is standing in front of hades. solid, real, alive.
you will yourself to move faster. something about this person is familiar.

he turns back to look at you.

you know him like you know yourself.
orpheus! how quickly had the underworld made you forget!

the two of you reunite.
