the echoes of your husband's songs follow behind as you turn away and move further into the Underground.

you walk.
and walk.
and walk.

until you can hear the whisper of a river, getting louder as you approach.

as you reach the banks of the river, frothy and whirling, you see a robed figure standing alone, lantern held aloft.
it turns to face you, beckons you aboard its boat, a rickety thing of dark wood. you know him like you knew yourself.

charon escorts you to the other side.
