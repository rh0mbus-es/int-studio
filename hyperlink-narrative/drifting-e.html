you meander for a while, letting the eddys and currents of your fellow shades move you along.
the chill of the underworld settles around you, resting heavy on your shoulders and inside your chest.
you're vaguely aware of a commotion in front of you, but the quiet whispers of the souls around you take over your senses.

you don't notice the human approach you until he rests his hand on your shoulder.

it all comes back to you. orpheus! how quickly has the underworld made you forget!

you reach out to him and reunite.
